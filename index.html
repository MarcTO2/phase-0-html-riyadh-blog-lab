History

Riyadh (Arabic: الرياض, romanized: 'ar-Riyāḍ pronounced [ar.riˈjaːdˤ] "The Gardens") is the captial and largest city of Saudi Arabia. It is also the captial of Riyadh Province. It belongs to the historical regions of Najd and Al-Yamama and is situated in the center of the Arabina Peninsula on a large plateau. It is home to 5,254,560 people and the urban center of a region with a population close to 7 million people.

The citi is divided into 15 municipal districts managed bt Riyadh Municipality, heady by Mayor of Riyadh, the riyadh Development Authority and chaired by the govenor of Riyadh Province, Khalid Bin Bandar Al Saud. The current Mayo of Riyadh is Abdul Rahman AJ Mogbel, appointed in 2012. Riyadh has the largest female university in the world, the princess Nora Bint Abudulrahman University. It has be designated as a beta World City
